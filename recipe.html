Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian pasta dish featuring rich, meaty tomato sauce served over al dente spaghetti. Perfect for family dinners or casual gatherings.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 15 minutes

Cook time: 45 minutes

Servings: 4 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 500g ground beef
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic, saut√© until softened.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking up clumps.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, and basil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Drain spaghetti and serve with the sauce on top.

Tips

Tip: For a richer flavor, add a splash of red wine to the sauce while simmering.
